DERMATOLOGY


The Department is well equipped with all facilities. All type of procedures like Radiofrequency Ablation, Peeling, Chemical Cauterization, PUVA, Phototherapyare done in the department. The department has separate ward of fifteen (15) beds for male and female patients.

Services-
  • Treatment of Hair Fall
  • Laser for Unwanted Hair
  • Wrinkles (Botox, Dermal Fillers)
  • Pulse Theraphy for Autoimmuine disorders

  • Pigmentation Face-Chemical Peeling (Glycolic acid, Salipeel,TCA peel)
  • Treatment of Acne Scar (Microdermabrasion, Dermal Fillers, PunchElevation)
