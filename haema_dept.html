HAEMATALOGY


Clinical Haematology is branch of clinical medicine which deals with diagnosis and treatment of blood & lymphatic system diseases. It includes both benign & malignant conditions including various blood cancers affecting blood cells, and stem cell transplant.
It includes:
  • RBC disorders.
  • Platelet disorders
  • WBC & plasma cell disorders.
