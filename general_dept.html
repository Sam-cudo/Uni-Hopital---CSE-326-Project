GENERAL MEDICINE


The Department has all types of facilities to take care for different types of diseases. The department has separate MICU of 13 beds with ventilatory support with multipara monitors. The department is fully capable of dealing with the critical patients with various conditions or emergencies like poisoning, hypoglycemia/ hyperosmolar nonketotic coma, CVA, ALD, CLD, Pancreatitis or MODS etc. Also, the department is supported by Dialysis units. The Department has separate 150 beds for male and female patients. The department have facilities of ECG and procedure for Diabetic Foot are also done here like Neuropathies, Doppler and foot wear specially made for diabetic patients
